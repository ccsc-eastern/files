Registration Information

Full Conference Registration includes the conference banquet on Friday evening, continental breakfast on Saturday, luncheon on Saturday, refreshments during breaks, admission to all sessions, and one copy of the proceedings.

NOTE: Early Registration closes on September 15, 2010

Registration Fees

Type Early     Regular     On Site
Standard $135 $155 $175
Student $35 $45 $55
K-12 Teacher     $50 $60 $70
*Vendor $150 $200 $200

*CCSC National Partners have no vendor fee

Programming Team Fee

$45 per team - includes Saturday Lunch for student participants. Students who are registered as members of a programming team may attend all regular conference sessions except the Friday evening banquet and pre and post-conference workshops. The team coach should register as a regular conference attendee.
